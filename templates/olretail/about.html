{% trans "About TimorMart" %}

{% blocktrans %}TimorMart is Timor-Leste's own online marketplace — a place for people across the country to buy and sell directly with each other, without needing a physical shop or a big budget to get started.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Why we built this" %}

{% blocktrans %}Retail in Timor-Leste is spread across markets, small shops and word-of-mouth — great for community, but hard for a buyer to compare options or for a small seller to reach customers outside their own neighbourhood. TimorMart brings that same everyday buying and selling online: a single, trusted place where anyone can list a product and anyone can find it, whether it's brand new or a good second-hand find.{% endblocktrans %}
